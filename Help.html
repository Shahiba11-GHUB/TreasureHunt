Help & Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place a bid?

Visit the View Items page, choose an item, click "Place Bid" and enter your bid amount.

How do I sell an item?

Go to the Sell Item page, fill out your item details, and list it for auction.

🛒 How does the cart work?

From the View Items page, add items to your cart. Review your cart anytime and proceed to checkout.

How can I contact customer support?

Visit the Contact page to send us a message. Our support team will respond shortly.
